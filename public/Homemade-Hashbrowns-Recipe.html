Homemade Hashbrowns Recpie

1. Olive oil in the pan just enough to cover the bottom
2. Get a cheese grater and peel the potatoes
3. Grate the peeled potatoes
4. Put the pan on the stove on medium to high heat and wait 1 minute or until you can't touch the pan
5. Put the grated potatoes into the pan
6. Get some butter and cut some up into 4 cubes
7. Put the cubed butter on the edges of the pan with the grated potatoes
8. sprinkle some salt on the grated potatoes
9. Wait until the one side is golden or crispy
10. Flip when golden or crispy
11. Sprikle some salt into the pan
12. When golden or crispy serve
13. Sit and enjoy
