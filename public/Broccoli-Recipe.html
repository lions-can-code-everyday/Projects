Broccoli Recipe

You will need Two Cloves of Garlic, Broccoli, One-Half Lemon, Olive Oil, Saute Pan, A Pot of Water, Red Chil Flakes

1. Boil the Pot of Water
2. When the Water is Boiling put the Broccoli into the Water
3. While waiting for the Broccoli to become vibrant Cut the Lemon into fourths
4. When the Broccoli is vibrant put them into the Saute Pan on Medium-Low Heat and put the Two Cloves of Garlic and some Olive Oil in too
5. When Olive Oil is bubbling squeeze two pieces of Lemon into the Saute Pan
6. Then put everything onto a Plate sprinkle some Red Chil Flakes on and Enjoy
