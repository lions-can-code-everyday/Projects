Smash Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients

3-4 potatoes (peeled, and diced into large pieces
2 garlic cloves, minced
4 tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste
Â¼ cup heavy cream
2 tbsp parmesan cheese

Equipment

Fork
Stockpot, boiling water
Strainer
Flexi spatula
Chefs knife
Paring knife
Cutting board
Bowl

Directions

Peel, dice potatoes, and boil on high for 10 minutes or until softened.
Drain potatoes in a strainer, and add cooked potatoes back into the pot
Mash with fork
Add salt, pepper, butter, and heavy cream to mashed potatoes
Put back on heat until butter melts completely, and stir with a spatula
Add parmesan cheese, stir again
Serve and enjoy.
